Leadership, Service, and Vision for District 321 C1

Lion Rajesh Kumar Singh

District Governor 2025-2026

Lions Clubs International — District 321 C1

Lion Rajesh Kumar Singh brings over two decades of dedicated service to Lions Clubs International. As the District Governor for District 321 C1, he leads with a steadfast commitment to the Lions' motto — "We Serve" — guiding clubs across Bihar to deliver impactful community service.

Under his visionary leadership, District 321 C1 has launched several flagship initiatives in healthcare, education, and environmental conservation. He believes in empowering every Lion member to be a catalyst for positive change in their community.

A respected professional and community leader, DG Singh has previously served as Region Chairperson and Zone Chairperson, earning multiple awards for outstanding service. His tenure as District Governor focuses on membership growth, youth engagement, and strengthening the Lionistic spirit across the district.
