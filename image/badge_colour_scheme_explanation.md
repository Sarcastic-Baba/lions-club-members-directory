
# Badge Colour Scheme and Design Explanation

## Overall Theme
The badge uses a **royal and prestigious** palette built around **gold, deep red, and navy blue**. The colours communicate:

- **Gold** → Leadership, excellence, generosity, and success.
- **Deep Red / Maroon** → Compassion, courage, and service.
- **Navy Blue** → Trust, stability, integrity, and professionalism.

The combination gives the badge a ceremonial and authoritative appearance while emphasizing service and compassion.

## Badge Structure

### 1. Outer Blue Ring
- Forms the boundary of the badge.
- Contains the name **"LION ADITYA GUPTA DG"**.
- The dark blue creates strong contrast with the gold text and gives an official appearance.

### 2. Inner Red Ring
- Contains the motto **"COMPASSION IN ACTION"**.
- The red colour symbolizes empathy combined with decisive action.
- Also includes district and year information.

### 3. Central Gold Circle
- Provides a radiant backdrop for the lion.
- Gold is traditionally associated with leadership and honour.

### 4. Lion Illustration
- The lion is rendered in multiple shades of gold and bronze.
- Represents:
  - Courage
  - Leadership
  - Wisdom
  - Service with strength

### 5. Lions International Emblem
- The blue-and-gold emblem at the bottom centre anchors the design to the Lions organisation.
- Blue and gold are the traditional colours of Lions International and symbolize loyalty and integrity.

---

## Design Impression
The badge follows a **classic heraldic style**:

- Strong circular geometry
- High-contrast colours
- Metallic gold emphasis
- Balanced use of warm (gold/red) and cool (blue) tones

Overall, the colour scheme communicates **compassion, authority, leadership, and service**, matching the motto **"Compassion in Action"**.
