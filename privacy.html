How we handle your information

Information We Collect

When you register on our platform, we collect information you provide such as your name, email address, club affiliation, designation, phone number, and profile photo. This information is used to create your member profile in the District 321 C1 directory and to facilitate communication among Lions members.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for the purposes of the Lions Club District 321 C1 community:

  • Displaying your profile in the member directory for fellow Lions to connect with you
  • Enabling community features such as the activity feed and event participation
  • Facilitating district-level communication and service coordination
  • Improving and maintaining the platform

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your profile information is visible to other authenticated Lions members within District 321 C1. We may share aggregated, non-personally identifiable data for district reporting purposes.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Authentication is handled through secure third-party providers (Clerk) with industry-standard encryption.

Cookies

Our platform uses essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics cookies. You can configure your browser to refuse cookies, but this may affect your ability to sign in.

Your Rights

You can access, update, or delete your profile information at any time through your profile page. If you wish to have your account removed entirely, please contact the District Governor's office. We will respond to data-related requests within 30 days.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out through the District Governor's Office.
